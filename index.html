Clients Question

Here are some common client questions that you might use to interrogate.

Our Equipment

Playing shirts are provided for you on match days. However, you will need to buy club navy shorts/skort, yellow and navy hockey socks.

Hockey Training

Joining a club or organization is an easy way to meet people who have the same interests as you.

Private Lessons

Your membership covers training and matches which can be paid in a number of payments across the season.

Ski Touring

The regular league season runs from the start of September to the end of March/start of April. We also run a Summer League from May to late Augus

Booking

We have a lot of people come to us who haven’t played since School as well as total beginners who want to give the sport a go. We cater for all! To get an idea of what our games are like, you can view our gallery. Please contact us or pop down to one of our games or training sessions and speak to our Captains.

Pricings

A lot of other clubs charge match fees in addition to the headline membership rate so over the course of the season the cost can mount up, this makes our all inclusive rates easy to understand and very competitive.
